Mission

Technology is disrupting the (life-long) education space and transforming the way people learn. Technology also accelerated the pace at which business challenges and content is changing. It has become necessary to establish a learning culture and environment to develop and retain talent that takes advantages of well-suited technologies.

Talk to us and let's find out together if there is anything that could be improved in your learning environment. Sometimes the implementation of a new gamification helps to start a transformation process. Sometimes we need to roll up our sleeves and design a whole new experience. Our mission is to get your learning experience ahead of the game.

Services

Blended Learning

The art of a true learning blend is a learner not thinking about different aspects of her learning plan. It is one experience - off- and online, mobile, collaborative, interactive and so on. We can help to design this experience with our innovative consulting approach.

Gamification

If you think Points, Badges and Leaderboards (PBL) are state of the art - you need to contact us. If you want to optimize your gamified enterprise - we're excited to see if we can help with our platform (Papa GAME©) and consulting services.

Interactive Video Learning

There are plenty of studies showing the great benefits of video based learning next to its economic advantages like scalabiliy. We can help to leverage the benefits and add on top with interactive, ego-perspective learning experiences (Papa LEARN©).

Data Driven HR

While everyone is talking about BIG Data at the moment we are already implementing it. Set a competitive advantage for your company in interpreting the vast amounts of data you already have. Our Learning Management System services can halp you to exploit the full benefits.
